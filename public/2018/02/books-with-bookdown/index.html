Books with Bookdown

February 12, 2018 in R

There is a large amount of free resource available to learn R. Once such resource is the books created by authors using the bookdown by Yihui Xie package. The bookdown package is built on top of R Markdown. Below I have created a table with those references that I had or plan to read. They go from a fantastic introduction to ggplot2 or data science to more technical in nature as the Advance R by Hadley Wickham. As I discoved more resources I will be updating the table. Happy learning.

Advanced R Hadley Wickham
Broadening Your Statistical Horizons J. Legler and P. Roback
Circular Visualization in R Zuguang Gu
Data Visualization A practical introduction Kieran Healy
Interpretable Machine Learning Christoph Molnar
Introduction à R et au tidyverse Julien Barnier
Mastering Software Development in R Roger D. Peng, Sean Kross, and Brooke Anderson
plotly for R Carson Sievert
R for Data Science Garrett Grolemund and Hadley Wickham

The Unix Workbench Sean Kross
